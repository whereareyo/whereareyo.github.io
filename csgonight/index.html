FAQ

When will the official launch of the nightly maps in CS:GO take place?

Estimated release date - the end of 2019.

How to start playing?

Now night maps are in beta testing. To start playing, you must log in via Steam to put your STEAMID64 into the beta test database.

How to add night maps to the map library?

You need to open the library of your games, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive - BETA VERSIONS - STEAMID64

What will I get for testing assistance?

All players who played on night maps will receive a special icon in the Steam "CS: GO NIGHT ASSISTANT" and a limited night background.
