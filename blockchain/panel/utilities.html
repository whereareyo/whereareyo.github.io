Rebalancing

When the market shifts, a portfolio will drift away from its target
allocation. HodlBot automatically rebalances your portfolio to
keep it on track. Portfolio rebalances generally lead to risk
mitigation and improved returns.

Cashout

Cash out allows you to convert any % of your existing portfolio
and consolidate it into a single coin. One use case, may be
cashing out to a stable coin in a bear market.

Coin Blacklist

Blacklist coins you never want to buy e.g. USDT. For Indices,
HodlBot will skip the coin and replace it with the next
